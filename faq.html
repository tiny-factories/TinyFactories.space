Who is this for?

Designers, coders and artists who are interested in owning their creative freedom and gaining financial independence by shipping products.


What do you mean by product?

It can be quite broad: from board games to web tools to things that support creativity.
In short, anything that you can sell!


Who is behind this?

Currently the main people conspiring this is Weiwei, William, Tomo, and Matt.

Why did you start?

We ourselves are trying to transition into launching indie products full-time and thought it would be interesting to join forces! More about why we started, read our blog posts here.


What’s the revenue sharing model for Tiny Factories?

Our first camp is self-funded. To encourage collaboration, we will agree on sharing revenue for the products shipped during the camp until it compensates everyone's living expenses made in the camp.

In the future, the living expenses during the camp will be funded by Tiny Factories for up to $2000 a month. To encourage collaboration, we will agree on sharing revenue for the products shipped during the camp until it covers the funding for the next batch.
